5.3 Line Spacing

The memoir class provides commands for single and double spacing (\SingleSpacing and \DoubleSpacing) most of your thesis will be double spaced, but certain parts (such as the table of contents, lists of figures/tales, bibliography) may be single spaced. This duplicates the functionality of the setspace package, which therefore should not be used. For bibliography spacing issues, see also Section 5.13.1.
